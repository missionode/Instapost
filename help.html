Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the background from my reference image show up?

Our engine explicitly commands the AI to suppress reference backgrounds. Ensure you are using a model that supports strict JSON blueprinting for the best results.

What happens if I don't have a logo URL?

Check the "If link fails..." box. The engine will name the requirements light_logo_upload.png. You can then upload your file directly into the AI chat.

Is my data saved?

Yes. Everything is saved locally in your browser. You can also use the Export button in the footer to download a backup of your brand configuration.
